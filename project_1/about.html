The Marvel Character Resource is a simple web app that utilizes the Marvel API. Users can search for their favorite character and retrieve their thumbnail as well as brief descripton of the character. Clicking on the series Featured Series button will display a short list of comic series featuring the character. Clicking on the Events Featuring button will display a list of notable comic events that have featured their character. All data is being provided by Marvel via the API
